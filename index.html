Features we offer

At E3 Multiplex, we take pride in providing you with an exceptional cinematic experience. Our commitment to quality and innovation ensures that your visit is not just about watching a movie, but about creating lasting memories. Here are some of the outstanding features we offer:

Comfortable Seat

Relax in our plush, extra-wide seats equipped with recliners, ensuring you have ample space to unwind while watching your favorite movies. Experience true comfort and enjoy the show like never before.

Dolby 7.1 sounds

Indulge in a culinary journey right at your seat. We offer a delectable range of snacks, meals, and beverages to tantalize your taste buds. From classic popcorn and nachos to gourmet dishes, there's something for every palate.

Online Ticket Booking

We provide a convenient and user-friendly online booking system that allows customers to browse movie schedules, select seats, and purchase tickets, through BookMyShow and Paytm.
